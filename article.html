Cebu Technological University Danao Campus: A Hub for Innovation and Excellence.

Cebu Technological University (CTU) Danao Campus is a premier educational institution in northern Cebu, recognized for its commitment to fostering innovation, academic excellence, and community engagement Nestled in the vibrant city of Danao. The campus is home to over 4,000 students who are dedicated to lifelong learning and career development in various fields of study, particularly in engineering, technology, and education.

Cebu Technological University (CTU) Danao is dedicated to fostering lifelong learning among its diverse community of around 4,000 individuals. The institution emphasizes a holistic educational experience, balancing academic excellence with wellness initiatives to promote a healthy and productive environment. With a strong focus on internationalization, CTU Danao facilitates opportunities for students and faculty to engage with top institutions globally, enhancing their learning and cultural exchange. The university is committed to continuous development, hosting extension programs and sporting events that encourage community involvement and personal growth. Through strategic leadership and innovative practices, CTU Danao positions itself as a premier institution ready to embrace future challenges and opportunities.

CTU CENTRUM: CTU Danao Centrum serves as a vital educational hub within the Cebu Technological University system, offering modern facilities and resources that support research, innovation, and academic excellence, while actively engaging with the local community through extension programs, workshops, and collaborative initiatives aimed at fostering technological advancements and entrepreneurial growth.

CTU GRANDSTAND/OVAL: CTU Danao Campus Director Rose Mary Almacen allocated 9 million for the rehabilitation of the Ramon M. Durano Sr. Memorial Sports Complex, generating excitement among local residents during the recent ribbon-cutting ceremony. This facility, which previously hosted the Southeast Asian (SEA) Games in 2005, boasts a stunning backdrop of limestone boulders against the clear blue sky. 5th District Representative Ramon “Red” Durano VI highlighted the potential for more opportunities at CTU Danao following the complex's restoration, emphasizing the benefits of having one of the university's 25 campuses in the area. As a sports enthusiast, Cong. Durano supports campus initiatives that promote health and wellness through athletic activities.

CTU FABLAB: CTU Danao Fablab, a University Shared Facility funded by the Department of Trade and Industries (DTI), aims to advance research and innovation in Engineering and Technology using state-of-the-art equipment, particularly in 3D printing. Officially launched on September 17, 2019, the Fablab engages faculty and students in digital fabrication, catering to various applications across industry, education, business, and art. Services include 3D printing and scanning, laser cutting, large format printing, and training in digital design for MSMEs, faculty, and students. Additionally, the Fablab has initiated extension programs to enhance digital application skills for MSMEs, start-up entrepreneurs, and community members, fostering improvements in business and education standards.
